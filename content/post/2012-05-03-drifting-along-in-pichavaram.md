Looking for a change of scenery? Cruise along the East Coast Road, pass Pondicherry, and take a break at Pichavaram, near the temple town of Chidambaram.

<div id="attachment_781" style="width: 1034px" class="wp-caption aligncenter">
  <a href="http://funderfulworld.files.wordpress.com/2012/05/dsc00083.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-781" title="Drifting Along" alt="" src="http://funderfulworld.files.wordpress.com/2012/05/dsc00083.jpg?w=1024" width="1024" height="529" /></a>
  
  <p class="wp-caption-text">
    Drifting Along
  </p>
</div>

Drift along in a boat along the backwaters, sit back and marvel at the beautiful mangroves of Pichavaram. The tranquil location is a haven for birds. It was our first glimpse of this amazing ecosystem, and we were thrilled! India has the distinction of being home to the world&#8217;s largest (Sunderbans) and second largest (Pichavaram) mangrove forests.
